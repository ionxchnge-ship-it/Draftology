Instructions

Here you will learn the study of the draft

  1. Pick a topic (ex: Best Actors, Musicians, literally anything)
  2. Enter your Room Code on your play screen
  3. Enter your name when prompted.
  4. When you're notified its your turn to draft. Enter your draft pick. (One at a time)
  5. Press the top left Queue button to enter your ideas to be drafted later.
  6. Vote for the best team by pressing the Left or Right button on the main screen.
  7. Thanks for playing.

Instructions

Here you will learn the study of the draft

  1. Once you enter your name, you can sign in as host at anytime in the game.
  2. You'll get access to a suite of buttons that'll make things easier.
  3. The Start button naturally starts the game, and if no topic is selected you can enter it remotely.
  4. It also can pause the game once it has started, and it can restart the game once someone is declared the champ.
  5. The Left and Right button will let you select those players as the winner and override the voting process.
  6. The Next button will let you skip players while they're drafting, or skip both voting timer.
  7. The Red Zoom button zooms the camera out and Green zooms in.
