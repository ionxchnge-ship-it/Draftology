Instructions

Here you will learn the study of the draft

  1. Pick a topic (ex: Best Actors, Musicians, literally anything)
  2. Enter your Room Code on your play screen
  3. Enter your name when prompted.
  4. When you're notified its your turn to draft. Enter your draft pick. (One at a time)
  5. When you press the top left Queue button, you can enter your ideas to be drafted later.
  6. When its time to vote press left or right button on the main screen.
  7. Thanks for playing.
